Hot Dogs

Description

What's more American than a tasty weener? Nothing.

The key to preparing a top tier hot dog is simple preparation and simple toppings. I like to smoke up the kitchen by pan frying them. I'll allow some creative license on the toppings, but don't overdo it.

Ingredients

  • all beef franks
  • saurkraut
  • mustard
  • white onion
  • mayonaise
  • buns

Directions

  1. split hot dogs lengthwise without fully separating, score rounded sides
  2. melt ghee in frying pan
  3. toast buns
  4. brown dogs in heated pan
  5. top to your heart's content
