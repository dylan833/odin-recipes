Red Lentil Soup

Description

This is one of my all time favorite recipes, perfect for a cozy night escaping the elements during Winter

One of the best things about this recipe is how quickly it comes together. As you can see below, the ingredient list is minimal, and the active cook time echoes that.

Ingredients

  • yellow onion
  • garlic
  • carrot
  • red lentils
  • tomato paste
  • chicken stock
  • spices
  • half a lemon
  • cilantro

Directions

  1. sautee garlic and onion
  2. add spices and tomato paste
  3. and liquid and lentils and carrot
  4. simmer 30 minutes
  5. top with cilantro and lemon juice
